# Strategic Brand Blueprint: Sustainer Vlakwerk ## Core Promise "You build the living concept. We deliver the physical 2D chassis. Delivered directly to the construction site, with 0mm tolerance, ready for immediate assembly." ## Brand Personality: The Museum-Grade Ingredient Sustainer Vlakwerk is a high-precision product supplier, not a service agency. The brand reflects this by being: * **Utilitarian:** Function over form. * **Precise:** Every pixel reflects the 0mm tolerance of the product. * **White-Label:** A canvas for others. Minimalist enough that any contractor (Trebbe, VORM) can see their own brand integrated with it. ## Visual Identity Principles * **Achromatic:** No colors. The "color" comes from the warmth of the LVL wood in photography. * **Grid-Obsessed:** Everything aligns to a strict technical grid. * **Technical Labels:** Use of small, capitalized sans-serif labels for sections (e.g., "01 / PRODUCT", "02 / PRECISION"). * **Iconography:** Minimalist line-art diagrams showing 2D elements. ## Key USPs to Communicate 1. **0mm Tolerance:** CNC-milled precision. 2. **Extra GO (Usable Area):** 3-5% more space due to thin LVL walls. 3. **Digital Twin Integration:** Direct link from model to factory. 4. **TCO (Total Cost of Ownership):** Focus on the financial total picture, not just m² price.
